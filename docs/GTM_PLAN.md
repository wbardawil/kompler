# Kompler — Go-to-Market Plan & Market Assumptions

## One-Line Pitch
"Stop getting surprised at audits. AI agents monitor your documents 24/7 and tell you what needs attention before auditors find it."

## The Problem (Validated)
- Uncontrolled documents and missing records are the #1 ISO audit finding
- Quality managers spend 10-20 hrs/week on document compliance manually
- One surprise audit finding can cost $50K-500K in fines, lost certifications, or production shutdowns
- In Mexico: IMMEX registrations are being cancelled for non-compliance, REPSE has strict 3-year renewal windows, new SEMARNAT environmental requirements
- 47% of workers find their document systems confusing and ineffective
- Companies that centralize documentation reduce audit prep time by 60%

## Target Customer (ICP)
- **Role:** Quality Manager, Compliance Officer, Operations Director
- **Company:** 50-500 employees, $10M-$500M revenue
- **Industry:** Manufacturing (beachhead), then healthcare, tax/accounting
- **Geography:** Mexico (LATAM) + US simultaneously
- **Regulations:** ISO 9001, IMMEX, REPSE, CFDI/SAT, IATF 16949
- **Current tools:** SharePoint, Google Drive, shared folders, Excel trackers
- **Budget authority:** $15K-$100K/year
- **Pain level:** Has been surprised at an audit at least once

## Anti-ICP (Don't Sell To)
- Companies with <10 documents (not enough value)
- Companies with dedicated IT departments running M-Files/OpenText (not switching)
- Air-gapped / no-cloud organizations
- Companies needing certification THIS MONTH (we're not a consulting firm)

## What Kompler Does (Product Promises)
1. **Daily Compliance Briefing** — Open the app, see a compliance score and exactly what needs attention today
2. **Proactive Alerts** — "Acme Corp supplier cert expires in 12 days, 3 POs depend on it"
3. **Contradiction Detection** — "SOP-042 says 180°C but WI-089 says 200°C"
4. **Knowledge Graph** — See how all your documents connect (Obsidian for business)
5. **Ask Anything** — Natural language Q&A across all documents (always free)
6. **Regulatory Monitoring** — Track IMMEX, REPSE, ISO audit deadlines automatically
7. **Expert Agent Marketplace** — Subscribe to expert-maintained compliance agent packs

## What Kompler Does NOT Do (Be Honest)
- We don't replace your document storage (we add intelligence on top)
- We don't guarantee audit pass (we reduce surprise findings)
- We don't provide legal advice (we surface what needs attention)
- AI is advisory — critical decisions need human verification
- We're not a workflow engine (we integrate with existing tools)

## Key Metrics to Validate (Discovery Calls)
1. **Pain frequency:** How often are they surprised by compliance issues? (monthly? quarterly? only at audits?)
2. **Time spent:** How many hours/week on document compliance? (>5 hrs = strong signal)
3. **Cost of failure:** What happened last time something was missed? (quantify the pain)
4. **Willingness to pay:** Show pricing → reaction (too cheap? too expensive? just right?)
5. **Trust in AI:** Would they trust AI to monitor compliance? (or need human-in-loop?)
6. **Agent marketplace interest:** Would they pay $49/mo for an expert-maintained IMMEX agent?
7. **Data sovereignty:** Do they need own LLM/storage, or trust SaaS?

## Discovery Call Script (30 minutes)

**Intro (2 min):**
"I'm building a tool that helps quality managers never be surprised at audits again. I'd love to understand your experience with document compliance to make sure I'm solving the right problem."

**Current State (8 min):**
1. "What's your role and what regulations do you deal with?"
2. "Walk me through your last audit. What went well? What surprised you?"
3. "How do you track document expiry dates and review schedules today?"
4. "How many hours per week does your team spend on document compliance?"

**Pain Depth (8 min):**
5. "What happens when something falls through the cracks?"
6. "Have you ever been surprised by an expired certificate or an outdated SOP during an audit?"
7. "How do you currently know if two documents contradict each other?"
8. "If I told you 'these 5 SOPs haven't been reviewed in 12 months,' would that worry you?"

**Solution Reaction (8 min):**
9. "Imagine opening an app every morning that shows you a compliance score and tells you exactly what needs attention — expired certs, overdue reviews, contradictions. What would that be worth to your organization?"
10. "Would you trust AI to read your documents and flag compliance issues?"
11. [Show pricing] "At $299/mo for unlimited users, proactive alerts, and a knowledge graph — how does that compare to what you spend now?"
12. "If an ISO expert maintained an AI agent that tracked every clause requirement for your industry, would you pay $49/mo for that?"

**Close (4 min):**
13. "On a scale of 1-10, how painful is document compliance for you today?"
14. "Would you be willing to try an early version of this tool with your actual documents?"
15. "Who else in your organization would benefit from this?"

## Survey Questions (Online — 7 questions)
1. What is your role? (Quality Manager / Compliance Officer / Operations Director / Other)
2. What industry? (Manufacturing / Healthcare / Tax-Accounting / Legal / Other)
3. How often are you surprised by compliance issues? (Never / Rarely / Sometimes / Often / Constantly)
4. How many hours/month do you spend preparing for audits? (<5 / 5-10 / 10-20 / 20-40 / 40+)
5. What's your biggest document management frustration? (Can't find documents / Don't know what's expired / Documents contradict each other / Preparing for audits takes too long / No visibility across documents / Other)
6. Would you pay for AI that monitors your documents and alerts you proactively? (Yes / Maybe / No) — If yes, how much? ($50-100/mo / $100-300/mo / $300-700/mo / $700+/mo)
7. Which regulations do you need help with? (ISO 9001 / ISO 14001 / IATF 16949 / IMMEX / REPSE / CFDI-SAT / FDA / HIPAA / SOX / Other)
