# Kompler Demo Script — Wednesday Meeting

## Before the Meeting
- Open Lovable app in browser
- Have 2-3 sample documents ready to upload live (SOPs, certs from their industry)
- Know: what frameworks do THEY follow? (ISO 9001? IMMEX? Something else?)

## Opening (2 min)

"Let me show you something. I'm going to upload a few compliance documents and in 60 seconds, you'll see exactly where your gaps are — clause by clause."

## Demo Flow (15 min)

### 1. Dashboard (2 min)
Show the dashboard:
- "This is a sample manufacturing company in Monterrey. ISO 9001, IMMEX, REPSE."
- Point to the score: "75 out of 100. Not bad, but not audit-ready."
- Point to framework bars: "ISO 9001 is fully covered. But REPSE is only 29% — that's a risk."
- Point to audit countdown: "78 days until the BSI audit."
- "The system found 2 issues. Not 50 items of noise — 2 real issues."

### 2. Compliance Map (5 min) — THE MONEY SHOT
Navigate to Compliance Map:
- "This is clause-by-clause coverage. Every green checkmark means we have evidence. Every gap is a risk."
- Show ISO 9001: "100% covered. Every clause has a document. The audit report maps to clause 9.2, the CAPA maps to 10.2."
- Show REPSE: "0% on mandatory items. No REPSE registration, no fiscal certificate, no IMSS compliance."
- "A compliance officer sees this and immediately knows: ISO is fine, REPSE needs work."
- Click a missing item: "It tells you exactly what's needed and where to find it."

### 3. Live Upload (3 min)
"Let me upload a document right now."
- Upload a real document (SOP, cert, or policy)
- Wait 10 seconds for classification
- "It classified it as [type], extracted [N] entities."
- Go back to Compliance Map: "See — this clause that was missing is now covered."
- "Score just went up. That's the loop: upload → gap closes → score improves."

### 4. Ask AI (2 min)
Go to Ask AI:
- Type: "what compliance risks do I have?"
- Show the concise answer: score, critical items, recommendation
- "This is free — unlimited questions, zero cost."

### 5. Action Items (2 min)
Show Action Items:
- "These aren't 50 generic alerts. It's 2 specific items with exactly how to fix each one."
- Show resolution buttons: "Upload renewed cert, or mark renewal in progress."
- "When you fix an item, the score updates immediately."

### 6. Close (1 min)
"This is $299/month, unlimited users. Your entire team — quality managers, compliance officers, the plant director — all use it.

Compare that to:
- A quality consultant at $200/hour
- 200 hours of manual audit prep
- Or the cost of one surprise finding

What would this mean for your next audit?"

## Questions to Ask THEM
1. "What frameworks do you follow? Is it just ISO 9001 or others?"
2. "How do you track document coverage today?"
3. "How long does audit prep take you currently?"
4. "Would you be willing to upload 10 of your real documents and see the results?"
5. "What would make you say 'I need this'?"

## If They Ask...

**"How accurate is the AI?"**
"Classification is 95%+ accurate. For critical fields like expiry dates, we flag them as 'AI-detected' until you verify with one click. We never say 'you're compliant' — we say 'here's your coverage.' The human makes the final call."

**"Where is my data stored?"**
"Cloud-hosted database. We store the analysis — classifications, entities, coverage mapping — not your original files. Your documents stay where they are."

**"What other frameworks do you support?"**
"We ship with ISO 9001, IMMEX, and REPSE. More frameworks are on the roadmap — ISO 14001, IATF 16949, FDA. We're also building a marketplace where compliance experts can add industry-specific frameworks."

**"How is this different from [competitor]?"**
"Most QMS tools are workflow engines — they help you MANAGE documents. We help you FIND GAPS. Think of it as Drata for document compliance: automated gap analysis, clause-by-clause evidence mapping, continuous monitoring. Upload your docs and see your coverage in minutes, not months."

**"What's the pricing?"**
"$99/month starter, $299/month pro with full framework coverage. Unlimited users. No per-seat pricing. Compare that to $12,000/year for Qualio or $200/hour for a consultant."
